Concerts & Cuisine

Plan a musical night out with this sleek and intuitive app! Enter your zipcode and let us do the planning for you - we'll find nearby concerts and and give you a list of delicious restaurants near the concert venue of your choice. Built collaboratively with a remote team


    Technologies:
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • Google Material Design/Materialize CSS
  • MapQuest, Songkick, & Zomato APIs
